ABOUT THE
GROUP

The OMG Girlz are a powerhouse American girl group from Atlanta, Georgia, celebrated for their unapologetically bold style, catchy melodies, and charismatic performances. Created in 2009 by music entrepreneur Tameka Tiny Harris, the group continues to influence youth culture and inspire a new generation of artists.
